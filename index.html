The DU Airlines Standard

Built like a training block, not a pep talk

Every athlete deserves a coach who sees more than today's performance. The goal isn't only to help athletes run faster or jump farther. It's to build the discipline, confidence, and mindset that carry into sport, school, and life.

Every session has a purpose. Every athlete has a plan. Every goal is treated with intention. As a 2× NCAA Division I Champion and current international athlete, Coach Du brings the same standards that earned a place on the world stage, and coaches each athlete exactly where they are today.

Great results come from a partnership between the athlete, the family, and the coach. When we commit to the same goal, progress follows.
